Sticky Header and Nav Bar

Sticky sidebars often contain navigation menus, links to important pages, or quick access to key sections of the website.

By keeping these options visible as the user scrolls down, it ensures that important content is easily accessible without the need to scroll back to the top.

Sticky sidebars improve user experience by providing a consistent and convenient way for users to interact with the site's features.

Users don't have to hunt for menus or links as they scroll through content.
