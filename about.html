Kirsten Groenewald

I grew up with two full time artists as parents. Design and art are in my blood.I took art through school and now as a hobby paint and draw, mostly using acrylics.

Having owned my own business for 12 years, I used my creativity as an outlet. I did all my website updates, took the photos and created social media content. I designed my adverts on Canva.

I have then since ventured into Figma and been loving the platform and it's diversity. I find that being creative is something I am passionate about and love diving into challenges and new projects as I have done over the past few months.
