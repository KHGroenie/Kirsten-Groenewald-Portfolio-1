04KH Fitness Gym App

Course project (June-September 2023)

    In this project, the idea was to come up with a concept and then design a prototype.

  • I went through the process of creating a persona profile for gym owner Kyle.
  • I did the same for Kyle's clients.
  • I then went onto the needs and expectations of Kyle and his potential clients.
  • Following that I created an empathy map for each persona.
  • From there I put together a site map for the app.
  • This allowed me to set up a user flow.
  • I then created a low-fidelity mock-up of the potential app.
  • A wireframe was set up.
  • Penultimately, I put together the colour palette and styles for the prototype.
  • I finished off with a prototype to test.

The prototype was reworked many times. I received insightful feedback along the way to improve my concept and design.
